Ann Arbor Arbetorium

Description

Ann Arbor Arbetorium is a great place for going for hikes and also for photography. One day early morning, my friends dragged me to this place at 5am just so, we could see the sunrise from this place. This picture is of that sunrise. The weather was early fall, late summer, perfect for hiking.

Camera settings

ISO 160, 26mm lens, f1.8, 1/60s

Date

Sept 26, 2021

Empire State Building at New York City

Description

Catching a glimpse of The Empire State Building from Highline at New york City was a great start to my new year. The weather was cold and breezy that day, when I had decided to not carry my overcoat. I am grateful for the time this picture was taken, it really captured the purple and orange coloured skies with this monumental building.
