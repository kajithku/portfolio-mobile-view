Scenic views

These are some of the scenic view photos I took over the years from various places I travelled.

Ann Arbor Arbetorium

Description

Ann Arbor Arbetorium is a great place for going for hikes and also for photography. One day early morning, my friends dragged me to this place at 5am just so, we could see the sunrise from this place. This picture is of that sunrise. The weather was early fall, late summer, perfect for hiking.

Camera settings

ISO 160, 26mm lens, f1.8, 1/60s

Date

Sept 26, 2021

Early winter late fall at Ann Arbor

Description

This is my first winter in Ann Arbor, and it has welcomed me pleasantly. This picture was taken in North Campus on the day of the first snow of 2021. That day was my birthday as well. I love how this picture has a glimpse of fall and winter in it.

Camera settings

ISO 160, 30mm lens, f1.8, 1/120s

Date

Nov 14, 2021

Empire State Building at New York City

Description

Catching a glimpse of The Empire State Building from Highline at New york City was a great start to my new year. The weather was cold and breezy that day, when I had decided to not carry my overcoat. I am grateful for the time this picture was taken, it really captured the purple and orange coloured skies with this monumental building.

Camera settings

ISO 125, 4.25mm lens, f1.8, 1/60s

Date

Jan 4, 2022

The Tunnel at New York City

Description

I visited The Tunnel, during the festive searon, hence I was able to witness it this lit up. I love how the reflection of the lights make the copper coloured tunnel shine even more. That day, this place was crowded as well as the Saks Mall accross the Tunnel. This day also marks the day I bought the most expensive peice of beautiful luxurious perfume I own, a 10ml bottle.

Camera settings

ISO 50, 4.2mm lens, f1.8, 1/60s

Date

Dec 31, 2021

Steam Chimney in Black & White

Description

This steam chimney building is at the junction of Huron and Glenn. This picture really brings out the details on the concrete walls of this building. The smoke emitted from this chimney is also very well captured in this picture. This black and white image is simple and textured.

Camera settings

ISO 25, 4.25mm lens, f1.8, 1/60s

Date

Dec 18, 2021

Taxis at New York City
